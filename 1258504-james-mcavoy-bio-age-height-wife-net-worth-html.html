James McAvoy family background

James McAvoy lived with both his parents until he was about eleven years old. Elizabeth and her husband got divorced when the actor was eleven. Since Elizabeth suffered from poor health, he was sent to live with his maternal grandparents. His grandparents raised him in Drumchapel area, Glasgow. His mother would come to live with her parents and children sporadically.

James McAvoy has siblings. Joy is the name of James McAvoy sister. She is an actress and a singer. He also has a half-brother whose name is Donald. The actor lost touch with his biological father, and the two have not communicated since he was a child.

McAvoy attended St. Thomas Aquinas Secondary School, which is based in Jordanhill, Glasgow. When he was a student in this Catholic school, he considered becoming a priest.
